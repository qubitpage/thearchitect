The ArchitectConstitution v2.1
Legal kernel

Perfect governance starts with rights that cannot be quietly bypassed.

Dignity and material security

Food, shelter, healthcare, education, connectivity, UBI, fair trial, privacy, and freedom from coercion.

Merit and anti-dynasty

Public roles, grants, promotions, contracts, and enterprise decisions must be transparent and competence-based.

Hyper-amendment

Kernel rights can evolve, but only through extraordinary democratic consensus and cooling periods.

Transparency Authority

A fourth branch dedicated to audit, corruption detection, and public accountability.

Emergency limits

No emergency can suspend dignity, fair trial, free press, GovLedger access, or core rights.

Planetary citizenship

Rights travel with the citizen across Earth, stations, vessels, and future settlements.

The ArchitectAI security
Lobster Trap DPI layer

Every governance AI call is inspected before and after the model.

Lobster Trap acts as a deep prompt inspection firewall: sub-millisecond rule checks, ingress and egress inspection, P4-style policy actions, human review, quarantine, rate limiting, and audit logs.

Protected AI pipeline

Ingress

Prompt injection, impersonation, secrets, exfiltration, malware, and obfuscation checks.

Policy

ALLOW, DENY, LOG, HUMAN_REVIEW, QUARANTINE, RATE_LIMIT.

Egress

PII, credentials, fabricated citations, harmful instructions, bias, unauthorized commands.

Audit

Structured logs linked to GovLedger and Transparency Authority oversight.
